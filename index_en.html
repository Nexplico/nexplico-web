About Us

Nexplico Ltd. is an Osaka-based technology company specializing in AI-driven solutions for the semiconductor and embedded systems industries. Led by a team of Ph.D. experts with over 20 years of R&D experience and numerous US patents, we provide high-efficiency optimization for next-generation hardware and software.

  • Intellectual Property: Extensive portfolio of US patents in system architecture and verification.
  • Global Partnership: Established collaboration with leading Taiwanese IC design houses.
  • Ph.D. Led R&D: Deep academic and industrial expertise in Computer Architecture and IC design.

Core Services
